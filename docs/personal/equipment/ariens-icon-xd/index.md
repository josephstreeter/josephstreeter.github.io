## Ariens IKON XD 60

The Ariens IKON XD 60 is a residential zero-turn mower built around a 60-inch fabricated deck and a 23 HP Kawasaki FR691V V-twin engine. The specific older XD model commonly referenced is model 915273, which appears in Ariens operator documentation for the 2019-2020 era IKON-XD product line.

This page exists as a quick reference for the machine itself, because a lot of generic product descriptions online leave out the details that actually matter when comparing or maintaining the mower.

## Overview

The IKON XD 60 was positioned as a step up from entry-level residential zero-turns. It combined a fabricated 11-gauge steel deck, a welded tubular chassis, and a foot-operated deck lift with a dial height selector. Ariens marketed the platform around durability, cut quality, and comfort for larger residential properties.

Key points that are easy to miss in generic listings:

- Model number for the Kawasaki-powered 60-inch XD variant is 915273.
- Engine is a Kawasaki FR691V V-twin rated at 23 HP with 726 cc displacement.
- Deck is a 60-inch, 3-blade fabricated deck.
- Cutting height is adjustable through 13 positions.
- Typical published ground speed is 7 mph forward and 3 mph reverse.
- Published fuel capacity for model 915273 is 2.8 gallons.

## Notable Features

### Fabricated deck

The 60-inch deck is a fabricated design rather than a stamped shell. Published descriptions for the XD 60 call out 11-gauge welded steel and a 4-inch-deep deck shell intended to improve airflow and cut quality.

### Chassis and frame

The mower uses a fully welded tubular steel chassis. Ariens product descriptions for the IKON family also call out closed tube frame rails, and reseller descriptions for the XD 60 reference 3-inch by 1.5-inch frame rails for added rigidity.

### Height adjustment

One of the more practical features is the pedal-assisted deck lift paired with a dial-style height selector. Instead of moving pins through a simple bracket, the deck can be set across 13 cutting positions in quarter-inch increments.

### Comfort and controls

The IKON XD 60 includes a high-back seat with armrests, premium steering grips, and zero-turn lap-bar controls. Ariens also emphasized the larger tire setup and seat comfort in the broader IKON family marketing.

## What Changed Later

Current Ariens product pages emphasize the newer IKON naming rather than the older IKON XD branding. If you are shopping for parts, manuals, or modern equivalents, it helps to search using both names:

- Ariens IKON XD 60

The older XD naming is still common across manuals, reseller pages, and parts sites, while Ariens' current product catalog is centered on the IKON series.
