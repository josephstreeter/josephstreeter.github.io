## Equipment

This section is a simple reference area for tools, equipment, and brands that I want to document for later. The goal is not to duplicate manufacturer marketing, but to keep practical notes, model references, and quick specifications in one place.

## Overview

The pages here are focused on real-world lookup value:

- model numbers
- engine and deck details
- configuration differences between product generations
- basic purchasing or maintenance reference information

Some entries are specific to a single machine, while others are broader brand pages that can expand over time.
